Client

This site is a graduate project of a Yandex student.Practicum's. Its purpose is to show what the student learned what technologies he owns.

The site layout is based on the BEM methodology. This makes the site flexible and scalable. In addition, adding new pages and functionality is easier and faster than in software projects other methodologies. The site is also easier and cheaper to maintain.

News cards come from the server and are processed asynchronously. That is the site it continues to work until a response is received from the server. This increases the usability of the service.

When creating the project, the Webpack build was used. It's modern approach to website development. The code is optimized, which means that the site runs faster. The site also works in older browsers. Even if the users are users of older operating systems, they won't have any problems with display. This means that the potential audience increases.

The project was reviewed by Yandex experts.Practicum's. If your business needs a website, we we recommend contacting this student.
